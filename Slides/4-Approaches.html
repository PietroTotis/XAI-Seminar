What do probabilities mean?

Two approaches[1] to define the semantics of probabilities in PAAF:
  1. Constellations approach: probabilities express uncertainty over the structure of the graph.
  2. Epistemic approach: probabilities express a degree of belief over arguments.
  1. A. Hunter, "A probabilistic approach to modelling uncertain logical arguments", 2013, Int. J. Approx. Reasoning

Probabilistic AAF

A Probabilistic Abstract Argumentation Framework (PAAF)[1] is a tuple $(A, R, P_A, P_R)$ where $(A,R)$ is an AAF, $P_A$ and $P_R$ are functions $P_A\rightarrow[0,1]$, $P_R\rightarrow A\times A \rightarrow [0,1]$.
  1. H.Li, N.Oren, T.Norman, "Probabilistic argumentation frameworks" TAFA 2011.

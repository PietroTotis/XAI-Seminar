Key Takeaways

    Modelling a joint probability (belief) distribution over arguments with PLP allows us to:
  • intuitively encode influences between beliefs over arguments;
  • infer the effect of these influences on the strengths of each belief;
  • apply to probabilistic argumentation PLP's reasoning and learning tools (evidence, mpe, lfi, ...).

What do probabilities mean?

Two approaches[1] to define the semantics of probabilities in PAAF:
  1. Constellations: probabilities express uncertainty on the graph's structure 2.
  2. Epistemic: probabilities express a degree of belief over arguments3.
  1. A. Hunter, "A probabilistic approach to modelling uncertain logical arguments", 2013, Int. J. Approx. Reasoning
  2. T.Mantadelis, S.Bistarelli, "Probabilistic abstract argumentation frameworks, a possible world view", 2020, International Journal of Approximate Reasoning .
  3. S. Polberg, A. Hunter, and M. Thimm, "Belief in Attacks in Epistemic Probabilistic Argumentation", 2017, Scalable Uncertainty Management

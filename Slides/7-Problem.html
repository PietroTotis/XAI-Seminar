Reasoning over belief (argument) graphs

Can we interpret the probabilities of arguments as degrees of beliefs1 and use (probabilistic) logical reasoning over the argument graph to find an agreement2 on the strength of each belief?
  1. Map argumentation graphs to Probabilistic Logic Programs
  2. Compute the joint probability distribution modelled by the program
  3. Have fun with PLP tools: conditional queries, most probable explanation queries, learning, ...
